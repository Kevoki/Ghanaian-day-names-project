Akan names

The Akan people of Ghana name their children after the day of the week they were born.

Below are the Ghanian names

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwa
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednessday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female

  • Sunday: Akusa
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednessday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
